LAYER ONE
THE DEBATE
Every Trade Is Cross-Examined
Before any position opens, Sovereign runs a structured 4-round adversarial debate. A bull model builds the strongest possible case. A bear model dismantles every assumption. They exchange rebuttals. An independent AI judge cites specific exchanges and rules on net conviction. Trades that can't survive cross-examination never reach execution. The strength of the pushback directly determines the size of the position.

L2 · REGIME FORECASTING
What Is the Market Going to Do?
Most systems detect what the market is. Sovereign tries to predict what it will be — classifying regime state and sub-variant in real time, tracking historical win rates per regime, and adjusting thresholds before the next shift. Each regime has its own playbook. The same setup that's high-conviction in a trending bull market is a pass in choppy compression.
L3 · COMPETING VARIANTS
Two Bots. One Allocation. Winner Takes More.
The equity bot and crypto bot don't get equal budgets — they compete for capital based on live risk-adjusted performance. The Commissioner allocates more to whichever bot is earning it. The losing bot trades smaller until it proves its edge again. Every dollar flows toward the strategy currently doing the most work.
L4 · SELF-WRITING
The System Rewrites Its Own Rules
A 19-step nightly pipeline reviews every decision made that day — entries, skips, exits, and blocked trades. It recalibrates signal weights, updates judge accuracy, identifies regime-specific blind spots, and proposes changes to its own behavior. Intelligence compounds every night. The system running today is always smarter than the system that ran yesterday.
L5 · CAUSAL MARKET
Acting on the Cause, Not the Effect
Sovereign connects macro events to individual positions before the broader market has priced in the relationship. Credit spread direction, tariff sensitivity, earnings flow, and cross-asset momentum are read as causal signals — not lagging indicators. The system acts when the reason is clear, not when everyone else has already moved.
L6 · COMPOUNDING SELF-KNOWLEDGE
The System Knows What It's Good At
Sovereign tracks its own behavioral patterns — which regimes it trades well in, which signals have the highest predictive power, where it has been overconfident. This isn't just P&L tracking. It's a running record of the system's own edge, updated continuously, so capital flows toward setups where Sovereign has demonstrated a real statistical advantage.
