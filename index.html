The Philosophy
"Every trade earns its execution."
No Free Passes
A strong chart pattern isn't enough. A good earnings beat isn't enough. Every proposed trade has to survive a structured argument from both sides before capital moves. The ones that can't hold up under pressure don't get in.
"The system that ran today is smarter than the one that ran yesterday."
Compounding Intelligence
Every night, Sovereign reviews what it did, what worked, and what didn't. It updates its own behavior based on outcomes — not fixed rules set once and left alone. The intelligence accumulates alongside the capital.
"Fix the intelligence, not just the trade."
Cause Over Correlation
When a position loses, the first question isn't "was the signal wrong?" It's "what did the system not know?" Every loss that traces back to missing context gets that context built in permanently. The system gets harder to fool.
