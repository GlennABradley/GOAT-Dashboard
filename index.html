Architecture
📊
AI Engine
The system’s opening read. Ingests regime data, sector rotation, macro pulse, on-chain signals, and multi-timeframe technicals. Generates a thesis, assigns confidence, and mandates an invalidation signal before anything else happens.
🥊
Devil’s Advocate
Every BUY survives a live adversarial debate before the Tribunal sees it. Bull case vs. bear attack. A pre-mortem maps 5 concrete failure modes. An Opus judge renders a verdict. Only setups that survive pre-screening reach the panel.
⚖️
The Tribunal
4-judge panel: Bear, Quant, Risk, and Regime. Each judge receives the DA debate results, regime win-rate calibration, and real failure history before deliberating. Bear holds hard-veto authority. Accuracy earns weight — every judge’s track record is live.
🎖️
The Commissioner
Capital allocator. Reviews both bots’ live performance daily and shifts allocation toward the stronger performer — like a coach adjusting playing time. The leader earns more capital. Operates in DUAL, EQUITY ONLY, or CRYPTO ONLY mode.
⚡
Execution
Kelly-sized positions, ATR-calibrated stops, live Alpaca order routing. A 29-step nightly pipeline grades every decision, updates signal weights, and compounds institutional memory. Each day the system knows more than it did the day before.
